Buckwheat Recipe

Description

Simple recipe that I regularly use to cook grechka.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup fried buckwheat
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Rinse buckwheat briefly in cool water
  2. Cover it with water
  3. Remove weeds
  4. Drain water
  5. Move buckwheat into pot
  6. Add 1 cup of water
  7. Tightly close the pot with a lid
  8. Quickly bring it to a boiling temperature
  9. Add a tip of tablespoon of salt
  10. Simmer on medium heat for 10 minutes
  11. Turn off the heat
  12. Leave the pot closed for another 10 minutes
  13. Stir buckwheat
  14. All done!
