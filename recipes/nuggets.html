Tofu Nuggets Recipe

Description

Crispy fried tofu nuggets that are easy and cheap to make.

Ingredients

  • 150 grams of firm or extra-firm tofu
  • 2 teaspoons of vegan flour
  • 1 tablespoon of curcumin
  • 2 pinches of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of sunflower oil

Steps

  1. Rinse tofu
  2. Cut tofu into 6-8 pieces
  3. Mix flour and curcumin
  4. Add 2 pinches of salt
  5. Roll tofu pieces in the flour-curcumin mix
  6. Pour sunflower oil into pan
  7. Fry pieces on a medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes on each side
  8. All done!
