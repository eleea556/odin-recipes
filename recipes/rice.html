White Rice Recipe

Description

Short-grain white rice recipe that we use in our family. Simple, straight-forward and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup short-grain white rice
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Rinse rice in cool water 5-7 times
  2. Soak it covered at a room temperature for 20-30 minutes
  3. Drain
  4. Put rice in a pot
  5. Add 1/2 cup of water
  6. Tightly close the pot with a lid
  7. Quickly bring it to a boiling temperature
  8. Simmer on low heat for 10 minutes
  9. Turn off the heat
  10. Leave the pot closed for another 10 minutes
  11. Stir rice
